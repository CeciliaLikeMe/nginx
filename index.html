Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes! All your notes are stored locally on your device using localStorage. They never leave your browser.

Can I use it offline?

Absolutely! Once installed as a PWA, you can use Note Book even without internet connection.

How do I install the app?

Look for the download button in the bottom right corner, or use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" option.

Can I share my notes?

Currently, notes are private to your device. You can copy and paste text to share if needed.
